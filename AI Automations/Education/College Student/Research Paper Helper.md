# Research Paper Helper

Helps you find sources, organize research, and track citations automatically for academic papers.

## What This Does

**What It Does:** Helps you find sources, organize research, and track citations automatically.

**How It Works:**
1. Searches multiple databases for sources
2. Saves articles and creates bibliographies
3. Organizes notes by topic
4. Checks for citation formatting
5. Tracks word count and progress

**What You Need:**
- Google Drive or Dropbox
- Library database access
- n8n automation tool
- Citation manager (Zotero is free)

---

## Key Features

### Automated Source Discovery
- Searches Google Scholar, library databases, and free research sites
- Finds related papers based on citations and authors
- Organizes sources by topic and relevance
- Creates automatic bibliographies in multiple formats

### Research Organization
- Creates folder structure for each paper
- Tags sources by section (introduction, methods, conclusion)
- Extracts key quotes and findings
- Tracks research progress and deadlines

### Citation Management
- Automatic citation formatting (APA, MLA, Chicago)
- Bibliography generation as you add sources
- Duplicate detection and source verification
- Integration with popular citation tools

## Cost Estimates

### Small Business (Individual Student)
**Monthly Operating Cost: $5-20**

**Breakdown:**
- n8n: Free tier (sufficient for personal research)
- AI processing: $5-15/month (abstract analysis and summarization)
- Citation tools: Free (Zotero) or $5/month (premium citation managers)
- Cloud storage: Free (Google Drive through school)

**Assumptions:**
- 2-4 research papers per semester
- 20-50 sources per paper
- Basic citation formatting and organization
- Personal academic research only

### Medium Business (Research Group/Graduate Students)
**Monthly Operating Cost: $50-150**

**Breakdown:**
- n8n Pro: $20/month (collaborative features)
- Advanced AI analysis: $30-80/month (detailed source analysis and synthesis)
- Premium citation tools: $20-50/month (institutional access and features)
- Collaboration tools: $10-30/month (shared research libraries)

**Assumptions:**
- Research collaboration across 5-15 students
- Multiple ongoing research projects
- Advanced source analysis and literature reviews
- Shared citation libraries and resources

### Enterprise (University Research Department)
**Monthly Operating Cost: $500-2,000**

**Breakdown:**
- Enterprise automation: $200-500/month (institutional deployment)
- Advanced research tools: $200-800/month (comprehensive database access)
- Citation management: $100-400/month (campus-wide licensing)
- Research coordination: $100-300/month (multi-project management)

**Assumptions:**
- Department-wide research coordination
- Integration with institutional research databases
- Advanced literature analysis and synthesis
- Multi-project research pipeline management

## Best Practices

### Research Quality
- Always verify AI-generated summaries against original sources
- Use multiple databases for comprehensive source discovery
- Maintain proper citation practices and academic integrity
- Keep organized notes linking sources to specific paper sections

### Academic Integrity
- Use automation for organization and discovery, not content creation
- Always read and understand sources before citing
- Check institutional policies on AI tool usage
- Maintain original thinking in analysis and conclusions
